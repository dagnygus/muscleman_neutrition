How do I pay for my order?

All about payment methods.

Here's how you can pay for your order:

  1. Payment in advance - the order is processed immediately after receiving the payment. We accept payments:
    • By bank transfer (both when shipping within the country and abroad) - currencies PLN, EUR, GBP, CZK
  2. Cash on delivery - you will pay for the ordered goods to the DPD courier in cash or by card upon delivery, or by card in a parcel locker.
